About the Application

Image Capture Application intuitively helps transform your paper-based business processes by leveraging best of breed OCR technology. It uses Microsoft Azure OCR services and to extract printed/ handwritten text images (including tables, nested tabled, key-value pairs, etc.) from images and multi-page PDFs documents. This information can be stored in structured databases and analyzed using various tools for trending, dashboards, and reporting.
